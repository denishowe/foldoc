FOLDOC source

The dictionary is a single plain text file. This and all the other files required to build the site are available in a tarball (2.3MB). Diffs are no longer available.

FOLDOC on PC/Mac/iOS/Android

NG-Computing include a version of FOLDOC in their Fora Dictionary product which runs on PC, Mac, iOS and Android.

FOLDOC in MobiPocket Dictionary format

Adam B released FOLDOC in MobiPocket Dictionary format for the iLiad.

Paper

FOLDOC is available on paper from your local printer, but 770,242 words would be about 2000 pages!
